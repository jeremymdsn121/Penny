Welcome to the neighborhood spot

Where Creedmoor comes for breakfast.

Casa Cafe is the kind of place where the coffee's always on and the tortillas are handled with care. We open before the sun's all the way up for the early crowd, and keep the griddle going through lunch with fajita plates, crispy tacos and the cheese enchiladas folks keep driving back for.

No reservations, no fuss — just honest Tex-Mex, friendly faces, and a plate that sends you off full. Pull up, dine in on the patio, or grab it curbside.
