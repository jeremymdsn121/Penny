**Madison Solutions LLC** ("we," "us," "our") operates **Penny**, a virtual
transaction-coordination service for real-estate brokerages. This policy explains what
information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices available. It applies to
the Penny web app, our messaging channels (WhatsApp, SMS), and our email handling.

**Last updated:** [date]

## Our role

When a brokerage uses Penny, the **brokerage is the controller** of the transaction data
it puts into the service, and **we process that data on the brokerage's behalf** under our
agreement and Data Processing Addendum. This policy describes our practices; a brokerage's
own privacy notices also apply to the data its agents handle.

## Information we collect

- **Account information** — the brokerage admin's name, email, and login credentials
  (authentication is handled by our identity provider).
- **Agent and party contact details** — names, email addresses, and mobile phone numbers of
  agents and transaction parties (buyers, sellers, lenders, title/escrow).
- **Transaction documents and their contents** — uploaded contracts and related files. These
  may contain **nonpublic personal information (NPI)** such as Social Security numbers and
  financial or income information.
- **Communications** — messages exchanged with Penny over WhatsApp, SMS, and email, and
  voice memos (which may be transcribed).
- **Usage data** — basic logs needed to operate and secure the service.

## How we use information

- To provide the service: extract contract fields, generate correspondence, track
  deadlines, checklists, and earnest-money receipts, surface compliance findings, schedule
  appointments, and message parties on the brokerage's behalf.
- To secure, maintain, and improve the service.
- To communicate about the service (operational/transactional messages).
- To comply with legal obligations.

We do **not** sell personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

## AI processing

Penny uses third-party AI providers (Anthropic; OpenAI for voice transcription) to perform
extraction, drafting, assistant, and compliance-support tasks. Document contents and
transaction data are sent to these providers solely to perform those tasks. Penny includes
an **AI-disclosure** feature so brokerages can inform transaction parties that an AI
assistant is involved, and an optional party-consent capture. [Confirm provider data-use
terms — e.g., no training on customer data — and reflect them here.]

## How we share information

We share information with **subprocessors** that help us run the service, each under a
contract requiring appropriate protection. Current categories:

- **Infrastructure & storage** — our database, authentication, file storage, and hosting
  providers.
- **AI providers** — for extraction, drafting, assistant, and transcription.
- **Messaging & email** — to send/receive WhatsApp, SMS, and email.
- **Property data** — comparable-sales/property lookups receive a property **address** only.
- **Optional calendar sync** — only if a brokerage or agent connects a calendar.

A current list is maintained in our Data Processing Addendum (Schedule C). We may also
disclose information to comply with law or to protect rights and safety.

## Data retention

Penny provides a **configurable document-retention policy** (default **7 years**,
broker-adjustable to match the brokerage's record-keeping obligations). Automated deletion
is **opt-in** and never runs without configuration. We retain other information as needed to
provide the service and meet legal obligations, then delete or de-identify it. On
termination, data is returned or deleted per the Data Processing Addendum.

## Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards including encryption in transit and at rest,
per-brokerage tenant isolation enforced by database row-level security, least-privilege
access, server-side-only privileged credentials, and private document storage. Our
infrastructure providers maintain SOC 2 / ISO 27001 attestations. No method of transmission
or storage is 100% secure. [State current SOC 2 posture as counsel advises.]

## Your choices and rights

Depending on your state, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal
information, or to limit certain uses. Because transaction data is controlled by the
brokerage, **requests from transaction parties are generally directed to the brokerage**,
and we assist the brokerage in responding. Account holders can contact us at the address
below. To opt out of SMS, reply **STOP**; for help, reply **HELP** or email support.

## Children

Penny is a business service not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect
information from children.
