Between contract and close

Every deal carries a stack of coordination work.

Deadlines to track, documents to chase, parties to keep in the loop, and any one of them can hold up a closing.

Inspection periods, financing contingencies, and EMD due dates live in too many places, and a missed one can hold up a closing.
