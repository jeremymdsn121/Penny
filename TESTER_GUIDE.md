# Welcome to Penny

Thanks for test-driving Penny, your brokerage's virtual transaction
coordinator. This is an early pilot — you're helping shape her. Here's how to get
the most out of it and what to expect.

## Getting started

1. Open the app at the link we sent you and create your brokerage account.
2. Walk through the short onboarding wizard.
3. Add a deal by **uploading a contract PDF** — Penny reads it and pulls out the
   address, parties, price, and key dates automatically. Give the extracted
   fields a quick once-over and fix anything she missed.

## How to work with Penny

The main way to interact is the **Ask Penny** chat on the home page. Talk to her
like you would a coordinator:

- "What's the status on 123 Main Street?"
- "What should I tackle first today?"
- "What's missing on the Oak Ave file?"
- "Draft a status update to the buyer on 123 Main."
- "Propose some inspection times for next week."
- "Pull comparable sales for 123 Main Street."

She can also review a contract for compliance, track your deadlines and the
documents in each file, and keep an eye on earnest money receipts.

**You're always in control.** Anything that leaves the building — sending an
email, booking an appointment, notifying a party, recording a compliance
decision — Penny drafts and then **asks you to confirm** before it goes out. She
never sends on her own.

## What's in this pilot vs. coming later

**Working now:** deal tracking, contract reading, the chat assistant, document
drafting, compliance review, comparable sales, deadlines, the document checklist,
earnest-money receipt tracking, and your broker dashboard/reports.

**Not in this pilot yet:** calendar sync, pushing listings to the MLS, and
e-signature (DocuSign). You'll see these labeled "not connected" — that's
expected; they're on the roadmap.

## A few important notes

- **Compliance review is an assistant, not legal advice.** Penny flags things to
  look at; she doesn't make a legal determination. Your judgment is the final
  word.
- **Earnest money is receipt tracking only** — Penny records whether the EMD came
  in. She does not do any trust-accounting math or move money.
- **Deadline reminders** run on a daily schedule we've set up for your pilot; you
  can also trigger a check yourself from the dashboard.
- **AI involvement is disclosed** on outbound emails when that setting is on.
